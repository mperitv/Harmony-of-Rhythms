# Harmony of Rhythms

One day, the rhythm of the celestial clock in heaven breaks down, and heaven begins to collapse. A human chosen by God is tasked with repairing this clock. Our character passes through purgatory to reach heaven and attempts to repair the clock along with its rhythm! And this is my first game!
 so I struggled a lot while making it, but I finally managed to create something. I hope you like it too. Since it's still very, very early, the assets aren't complete yet. I'll draw the character as soon as possible! The background animation will be ready very soon!!!!

## About

**Harmony of Rhythms** consists of two parts:

1. **Purgatory** — Our character moves towards the celestial clock in front of her, and in the process, creatures around her attack her.
2. **Heaven**  — Our character saves heaven with her own rhythm!



## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `D` | Left melee |
| `K` | Right melee |
| `F` | Upper-left |
| `J` | Upper-right |
| `Space` | Jump |

For prototype: 
D for Red Squares
F for Blue Squares 
J for Yellow Squares
K for Green Squares
Space for Purple Squares
